QuotaBar Privacy Policy

QuotaBar is designed to minimize data collection. The current App Store surface in this repository does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking data.

Data Collection

The review/demo build in this repository uses deterministic local fixture data. No personal data is transmitted to third parties in demo mode.

Tracking

QuotaBar does not track users across apps or websites.

Future Live Sync

If a live App-Store-safe data source is added later, this policy and the privacy manifest must be updated before submission.

Contact

Open an issue in the repository support channel for questions about this policy.
